Mountaineer Animal Rights Chapter

"Veganism is a philosophy and way of living which seeks to exclude—as far as is possible and practicable—all forms of exploitation of, and cruelty to, animals for food, clothing or any other purpose; and by extension, promotes the development and use of animal-free alternatives for the benefit of animals, humans and the environment. In dietary terms it denotes the practice of dispensing with all products derived wholly or partly from animals."

Who we are

Morgantown's community of vegans and those aspiring to live a vegan lifestyle

Activism

We do community service, tabling, outreach, movie screenings.

Support

Veganism is not a widely accepted ideology, we want to provide a community where vegans can be themselves and help each other from burn out.

SURGE

Earthing Ed's Organization SURGE provides us with outreach material and funding. He has wonderfully informational videos on YouTube if you have not seen them.

How to join?

There are no dues or requirements to be apart of Monarch. Contact us on Instagram or by email to get connected with other vegans today!

Meetings

We strive to have meetings weekly or bi-weekly, usually in the Mountainlair
